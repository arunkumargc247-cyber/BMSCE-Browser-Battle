APEX Unveils State-of-the-Art Heavy Robotics Facility

The Mechanical Engineering department has officially inaugurated its "Advanced Industrial Automation Lab" featuring industrial-grade 6-axis KUKA robotic arms.

The lab, established with a $2M grant, aims to bridge the gap between academic theory and industrial application. Students will now have hands-on access to technologies used in Tesla, SpaceX, and BMW assembly lines.

"This isn't just a lab; it's a factory of the future. Our students will graduate not just as engineers, but as masters of automation, ready to lead the fourth industrial revolution." — Professor Sarah Jenkins, Head of Robotics

The facility includes specialized zones for machine vision, haptic feedback research, and collaborative robotics (Cobots), ensuring that APEX remains at the forefront of global engineering research.
