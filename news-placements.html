Record Breaking 65 LPA Package Secured by Data Science Trio

In a historic milestone for APEX University, three final-year students from the Department of Data Science have secured international placements with a staggering package of 65 Lakhs Per Annum (LPA).

The students were recruited by a premier London-based Fintech firm following a rigorous five-round selection process that tested their expertise in predictive modeling, algorithmic trading, and large-scale data architecture.

"Our curriculum's focus on real-world industrial datasets gave us the edge needed to solve the complex high-frequency trading problems presented during the hackathon round."

The university’s placement cell noted that this year has seen a 40% increase in high-ticket international offers, cementing APEX's reputation as a global talent hub.

This achievement reinforces APEX University's position as a leader in technical education and industry readiness.
